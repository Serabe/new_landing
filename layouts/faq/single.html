For Developers

Introducing

What is Tyba?

Tyba is the company behind source{d}. It was started in 2011 in Madrid, Spain.

What is source{d}?

source{d} is a product by Tyba that analyses all open-source repositories to match developers with job offers that actually match them.

How are you different from a recruiter?

Recruiters spam you with irrelevant offers because they are not able to understand a developer profile. source{d} has been built by developers for developers to contact you only with job opportunities that match your skills and experience.

Will it cost me anything?

No, we don't charge developers a dime.

What do you do with my data?

We only use it internally for recruitment purposes. We never give it to our clients nor anyone. We started this because we hate recruiters who disrespect your inbox as much as you do, but we also hate companies who use people's data in the wrong way as much as you do.

Process

How does the process look like exactly?

Once we've matched you to one of our offers we contact you to know if you'd be interested in the offer. If that would be the case we're more than happy to provide as much information as you might need and we will schedule a phone call to solve any other doubts and have a quick discussion. Some clients also ask us to do a technical test. After that, if we still consider you a good fit, we will send your contact details and a summary of your profile to our client for him to get in touch with you.
